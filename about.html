Established 1961 A.D.

Founded by local residents and ex-army personnel, our school journeyed from a cow shed to the largest community school in Nepal. We operate in two sectors: Kalikanagar (Sector-1) and Ramnagar (Sector-2).

Why Choose Us?

Inclusivity: Free hostel facilities for orphans and students from remote areas.
Recognition: Awarded 5 National Flags and the British Council ISA Certificate.
Methodology: Student-centered learning with modern Montessori and Smart Labs.
